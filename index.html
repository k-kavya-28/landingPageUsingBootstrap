Apple

Apple Inc. is a publicly traded company, so no individual or entity owns the company in its entirety. However, the majority stakeholder of the company as of 2023 is The Vanguard Group, which holds 7.6% of all outstanding shares.

Facebook

Facebook dominates social media through its family of apps that include Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger, and the original Facebook. The company, led by founder and CEO Mark Zuckerberg, debuted in 2004 as a social network for Harvard University students, one of whom was Zuckerberg himself.

Google

Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. That's why Search makes it easy to discover a broad range of information from a wide variety of sources.

FAANG is an acronym used to describe some of the most prominent companies in the tech sector. Originally the acronym was FANG for Facebook, Amazon (AMZN 1.21%), Netflix (NFLX -0.66%), and Google. In 2017, investors started including Apple (AAPL 0.48%) in the group, turning the acronym into FAANG.
